Health Insurance
Questions, Answered.

How much does health insurance cost?

It depends on your age, household size, plan type, and where you live. If you qualify for a subsidy, it can significantly lower your monthly premium. A licensed advisor can give you a real, zip-code specific number in minutes.

Where can I buy health insurance near me?

Our network of licensed advisors covers every state, so wherever you live, we can show you the health insurance plans and carriers actually available in your area - no local office visit required.

What's the difference between Marketplace and private health insurance?

Marketplace (ACA) plans are the only place you can use a subsidy to lower your monthly cost. Private, off-exchange plans skip the subsidy but can offer broader carrier and network choice. An advisor can compare both for your situation.

Can I get health insurance if I'm self-employed?

Yes. Freelancers, 1099 contractors, and small business owners without an employer plan have the same access to Marketplace, private, and short-term options as anyone else.

How do I know which plan is right for my family?

A licensed advisor will walk through your household size, budget, and which doctors you want to keep, then compare family plan options side by side before you enroll.

Is the Leo Source advisor service free?

Yes. Our concierge advisors are compensated directly by carriers - there's no separate fee, retainer, or commission added to your premium, subsidized or not.

Who does Leo Source serve?

Leo Source focuses exclusively on Americans ages 18-64, helping people find the right health insurance plan for their situation and budget.
